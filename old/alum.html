Mission
The Carnegie Mellon University alpha Kappa Delta Phi Alumnae Association's mission is to provide an organization to promote the continuation of the values of sisterhood, scholarship, leadership, and Asian American awareness for its alumni through collaboration with the Carnegie Mellon University alpha Kappa Delta Phi Colony Chapter, the Office of Alumni Relations, and the alpha Kappa Delta Phi National Alumnae Association.
Vision Statement
Carnegie Mellon University alpha Kappa Delta Phi Alumnae Association will be a premier network of innovative female leaders through:
  1. Promoting the continuation of the values of alpha Kappa Delta Phi Sorority, Inc after completion of their time as active members of Carnegie Mellon University;
  2. Providing and Promoting opportunities for volunteer engagement, professional association, career development, leadership and fellowship among Carnegie Mellon University alpha Kappa Delta Phi Alumnae;
  3. Facilitating support, mentorship and communication for Carnegie Mellon University alpha Kappa Delta Phi Alumni and Active Chapter;
  4. Sustaining a network among Carnegie Mellon University alpha Kappa Delta Phi alumnae with alumnae from other chapters;
  5. Encouraging alumni to contribute their time, talent and financial support to the Carnegie Mellon University alpha Kappa Delta Phi Active Chapter.
